Easy Lasagna Cups

Description

These easy lasagna cups taste like the crunchy delicious edges of a lasagna. Made with inside-out pasta shells, filled with meat sauce and cheese, they're a terrific 2-bite lasagna appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1(24 ounce) jar prepared marinara sauce
  • 4 teaspoons kosher salt divided
  • 3/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a mini muffin pan.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil over high heat.
  3. Heat a large deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and cook, crumbling with a spoon, until browned, 5 to 7 minutes; drain any excess grease. Add onion and cook until softened, 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in marinara, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat, and cook for 20 minutes, stirring often.
  4. Add remaining 3 teaspoons salt and pasta to boiling water and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender, 10 to 12 minutes. Drain, rinse with cold water, and drain well.

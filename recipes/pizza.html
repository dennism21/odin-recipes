Pizza

Description

This pizza uses hummus instead of the usual red sauce—a unique and healthy pizza for those bored with the traditional. Top with your favorite veggies and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 can pizza curst
  • 1 cup hummus spread
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack chesse

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees
  2. Roll out pizza crust. Shred cheese
  3. Bake in preheat oven until golden crust
